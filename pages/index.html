Welcome!

With a variety of popular JavaScript tools, more than twenty websites, and millions of users all over the world, there's always more to be done in the world of jQuery. From code and documentation to web design and support, the jQuery Foundation welcomes contributions from anyone willing to put in the time and effort to help us and our community of users. Whether you're a grizzled open-source veteran or a curious newcomer, if you want to get involved, use this site to find out how!

Sign Up!
